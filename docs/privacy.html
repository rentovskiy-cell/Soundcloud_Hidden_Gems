Privacy Policy

SoundCloud Hidden Gems · Last updated: June 25, 2026

Summary: This extension processes your SoundCloud likes only on your device. We do not operate a backend, do not sell data, and do not use analytics or advertising trackers.

SoundCloud Hidden Gems ("the Extension") is an unofficial browser tool. It is not affiliated with SoundCloud.

What data the Extension accesses

When you choose to load your likes, the Extension:

  • Reads your liked tracks from SoundCloud using your existing browser session on soundcloud.com
  • Uses SoundCloud API endpoints with credentials already present on the page — the Extension does not ask for your password separately
  • Reads track metadata: title, artist/uploader, plays, followers, duration, tags, URLs, and like date

What data is stored locally

All of the following stays in your browser:

  • Likes cache — fetched likes for faster reopen
  • Seen tracks — IDs previewed during digging
  • My Gems — starred track IDs
  • Filter presets — saved filter settings
  • Session gems — temporary stars (in memory until cleared)

Clear data via Chrome extension settings or uninstall.

What we do not do

  • No transmission to our servers — we have none
  • No third-party analytics or advertising
  • No sale or sharing of personal data

Network requests

Only soundcloud.com and api-v2.soundcloud.com — to fetch likes and control playback.
